Useful links

  • NBLAST tool: find similar neurons and GAL4 drivers that label specific neurons
  • BrainTrap: database that contains database information on protein expression in the adult brain
  • FlyBase: a database of Drosophila genes & genomes
  • FlyCircuit: database for online archiving, cell type inventory, browsing, searching, analysis and 3D visualization of individual neurons in the adult brain
  • FlyLight: database of the expression patterns of GAL4 and LexA Driver Lines from Janelia Research Campus
  • FlyBrain Neuron NDB: database of neuron, strain, antibody and brain regions data.
  • Interactive maps of embryonic and larval brain (by Christof Rickert, University Mainz)
  • Jefferis lab
  • Standard nomenclature of the adult brain (Ito et al., 2014)
  • Protocols for immunostaining, imaging and image registration (Ostrovsky et al., 2013).
  • External Resources from FlyBase: a comprehensive list of resources for fly researchers, including CRISPR, RNAi and antibodies links.
